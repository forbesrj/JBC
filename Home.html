What is Just Better Code? It's like browsing the web, just better

Have you ever been to a web site and wondered, "What is this site for?", "Where do I want to click?", "What do I want to do?", or anything at all? Just Better Code's mission is to remove any questions from the user experience (UX) in all software so that they can quickly and easily navigate to where they want to go and even places the user did not know they wanted to go when they arrived.

For better code, you need the best tools. And a very large tool belt.

Just Better Code uses Agile methods to determine the right tools for the job. Meaning find the tool that works for the task at hand with the lowest impact and investment. This way if and when the requirements change, we are ready to change with them. Some of the technologies we have used to achieve prior goals are: AngularJS, KnockoutJS, BackboneJS, jQuery, jQueryUI, Bootstrap, HTML5, AJAX, Razor, C#, MVC, TeamCity, nUnit, Git, TFS, JavaScript, CSS, ASP, PHP, JSP, and Java.

Nacho Business

Nacho Business is a prototype AngularJS online ordering system. The key features are custom routing, responsive display, a menu based on a factory, and customized views based on inventory.

Body Mantra

Body Mantra is a Wordpress CMS site for a massage therapy business in the Roaring Fork Valley of Colorado. It features a customized theme and a custom responsive slideshow widget.
